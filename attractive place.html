ATTRACTIVE PLACE IN BANDAR KUALA PILAH
TOKONG SI THIAN KONG
Elevating the spirits of the Chinese community this Chinese New Year, the magnificent Si Thian Kong Temple in Kuala Pilah has become a popular tourist destination in the state due to its uniqueness and an abundance of colorful neon and LED lights. The house of worship is a symbol of the Chinese community's pride and has historical significance of its own. Seeing the panorama at night through the building's many colorful lights is more captivating. Over 2,000 lanterns were hung throughout the temple grounds, and the vibrant light also accentuatedthe decorations on the tiger statue and other items.
Lot PT 2157, 7KM Jalan Tampin, 72000 Kuala Pilah, Negeri Sembilan Darul Khusus.
ULU BENDUL RECREATIONAL PARK
Ulu Bendul Recreational Forest is a popular location for day trips and picnics because it's quiet and easily accessible. In Negeri Sembilan, the recreational forest lies in the area between Kuala Pilah and Seremban. Angsi Forest Reserve is where it is tucked away. Wading along the meandering stream or swimming in the pond, Ulu Bendul is surrounded by lush natural vegetation. The river is lined with boulders, which prevent the water from flowing naturally and instead cause it to flow quickly, almost like a natural jacuzzi, relieving tension and massaging exhausted muscles! To let the cascading water's healing effects, simply recline against the rock.
TAMAN REKREASI, 71500 ULU BENDUL, Negeri Sembilan
Tel : 012-374 2837
ISTANA SERI MENANTI
The palace that is commonly mentioned in this context is an ancient, wooden structure that is still standing and was used as the Yang di-Pertuan Besar of Negeri Sembilan's official residence in the early 1900s. The palace's "role" has now been changed to museum since it is a historical landmark. The architecture of the Minangkabau community was taken into consideration when constructing this structure.
Muzium Diraja Istana Lama Seri Menanti, 71550 Seri Menanti, Kuala Pilah
