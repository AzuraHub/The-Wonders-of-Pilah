SCHOOL IN KUALA PILAH

In Kuala Pilah, there are 62 schools in total, including national schools, national type schools (Chinese and Tamil), national high schools, religious junior high schools and science high schools. Some of the famous schools in Kuala Pilah are:
SEKOLAH MENENGAH AGAMA SAINS KUALA PILAH SEKOLAH MENENGAH SAINS TUANKU JAAFAR SEKOLAH MENENGAH KEBAANGSAAN ZABA
Sekolah Menengah Agama Sains Kuala Pilah or SMA Sains Kuala Pilah is located in Johol, Kuala Pilah, Negeri Sembilan. The opening of this school was officiated by YAB Dato' Seri Utama Tan Sri Haji Mohd Isa bin Haji Abdul Samad, former Menteri Besar of Negeri Sembilan on 11 October 2001 and Malaysia was operational on 8 March 2001 with the first generation of 81 students in Form 4.
Address:Kuala Pilah,73100 Johol, Negeri Sembilan
Phone:06-4367510 		Sekolah Menengah Sains Tuanku Jaafar (SMSTJ or STJ) is a boarding school (SBP) located about 2km from the town of Kuala Pilah, Negeri Sembilan. Tuanku Jaafar Science High School (STJ) was established in 1973 and was completed in 1975. Its establishment aims to provide science education to bumiputera students, especially from the countryside.
Address:Jalan Kampung Gentam, Taman Fatimah, 72000 Kuala Pilah, Negeri Sembilan
Phone:06-481 1304 		Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Za'ba or SMK Za'ba for short, is a national high school located in Jalan Tampin. In 2009, Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Za'ba had 337 male students and 157 female students, making a total of 494 students. It has a total of 63 teachers.
